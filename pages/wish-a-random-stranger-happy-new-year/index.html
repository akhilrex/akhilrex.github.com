Last week i built a small fun tool where you get to answer random questions of strangers on twitter. It was a fun project and I really enjoyed building it. Now since today it is 31st December 2012, i think that this tool can be modified into a new New Year specific fun tool as well.

This app will pull random New Year wishes from twitter and you will get a chance to reply to that person's wish. The wish will change every 45 seconds if you do not answer. If you do not like the wish(???) or do not have an answer you can simply choose to skip to another wish.

You could find some really awesome wishes here. If you like the app, do share. Have fun!
